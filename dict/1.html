新説解　上卷

一 Ideogram (指事): ONE stroke.
丁 Pictogram (象形): a nail.
Phonetically borrowed character (假借): original form of 釘.
七 Pictogram (象形): one stroke cutting through another.
Phonetically borrowed character (假借): original form of 切.
万 Pictogram (象形): a person dancing.

Ideogrammic compound (会意): 一 ("one"), indicating a number, with pictogram (象形) of a person advancing; a large numeral.
(萬)
丈 Ideogrammic compound (会意): 十 (“ten”) with 又 (“hand”); ten chi (尺) is one zhang (丈).
三 Ideogram (指事): THREE strokes.
上 Ideogram (指事): a line ABOVE another.
下 Ideogram (指事): a line BELOW another.
不 Pictogram (象形): the calyx of a flower.
Phonetically borrowed character (假借): original form of 柎.

Pictogram (象形): a bird flying toward the sky (一); the sky being the limit for the bird, thus the idea of negation.
与 Specialization (轉注): from 牙, the semantic component of the traditional form.
(與)
丏 Pictogram (象形): a parapet for warding off arrows, thus implying concealment.
丐 Pictogram (象形): a person leaning forward to ask for help.
丑 Pictogram (象形): a hand, graphically depicting the final phase of the moon.
且 Pictogram (象形): a stone altar.
Phonetically borrowed character (假借): original form of 祖.
世 Pictogram (象形): three leaves on a branch.
Phonetically borrowed character (假借): original form of 枼.

Ideogrammic compound (会意): triplication of 十 (“ten”) - thirty, such as a variant form 丗. Thirty years make one generation (thirty times twelve months gives 360 months).
丘 Pictogram (象形): a hill.
丙 Pictogram (象形): unknown meaning.
丛 Ideogrammic compound (会意): 一 (“ground, floor”) with 从 (“two people standing together”); to make cluster.
(叢)
业 Pictogram (象形): a sawtooth-shaped board. Simplified from the traditional form via elimination of the lower components.
(業)
丞 Ideogrammic compound (会意): 卩 (“kneeling person”) with 廾 (“both hands”) and 凵 (“hole”); to rescue a person from a hole.
丢 Ideogrammic compound (会意): 一 (“one (item)”) with 去 (“to go; to remove”).
(丟)
並 Ideogrammic compound (会意): 立 beside 立; to put things side by side.
