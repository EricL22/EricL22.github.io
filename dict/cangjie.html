新説解　仓頡整理方法

This dictionary makes use of a a modified Canjie encoding method to collate its entries. Certain letters have been split into sub-indices as shown below if the shapes of subcomponents are deemed sufficiently different, but they are still typed using the single parent letter. Code collisions are resolved by order of frequency.
